What We Do

Headquartered in Richmond, Virginia, McCann teams with institutional capital sources to acquire, develop, construct and operate apartment communities in the Southeast, Southwest, and Mid-Atlantic regions. McCann and its principals have decades of experience and a strong track record in the apartment industry.

Acquisition & Development

Since inception, McCann has acquired 32 multifamily communities totaling more than 8,700 units in transactions valued at over $880 million. McCann has also completed or has under development 11 communities totaling 3,352 units with an estimated completed value of $450 million.
